Parking

Find the lot that's right for you

Long Term Parking

For the travelers that will be parked for more than a day we ask for you to park in the red lot. There are security cameras and patroling security, but any theft will be the responsiblity of the car owner.


Fee Rate: $12/day

Day Parking

For customers that need day parking, you will want to park in the green lot. For your convenince, we offer shuttles to check-in desks, luggage, and pickup.


Fee Rate: $6 for the day

Pickup/Dropoff

If you are coming for a quick pickup or dropoff, we have a desinated parking lot for you. Because of high traffic, we ask that you do not take more than 30 mintues in this lot. If you are in the lot for more than the alloted time, you will need to pay a fee.


Fee Rate: $5/hr
